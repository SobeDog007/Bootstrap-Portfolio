About Me

Hi! My name is Steven and I'm part of the UNC Boot Camp class! I'm a software developer with a background mainly in C++ applications for the defense and retail industries. I graduated from Clemson University in 2004 with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Engineering. I've lived in the Triangle area for over six years.

Outside of work and class, I manage and play on two indoor soccer teams in Morrisville. I'm also on the local leadership team of a national charity called Extra Life that raises money for the Children's Miracle Network through playing video and board games.
